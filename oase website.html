Before any drip is administered, we conduct a full initial medical assessment. Through blood analysis and metabolic evaluation, we map your body's unique state — oxidative stress levels, vitamin deficiencies, hormonal balance, and cellular function. Only then do we design your personalised protocol.

Why IV Therapy

The science behind the serum

Unlike oral supplements, intravenous therapy delivers nutrients directly into your bloodstream — achieving up to 100% absorption that the digestive system simply cannot match. The results are felt faster, more fully, and with a precision that oral administration cannot replicate.

  • Immediate cellular hydration and nutrient delivery
  • Boosts energy levels and reduces chronic fatigue
  • Strengthens immune response and reduces inflammation
  • Supports skin radiance, collagen production and anti-ageing
  • Enhances athletic recovery and reduces muscle soreness
  • Improves mental clarity, focus and cognitive performance
  • Detoxifies the body and optimises metabolic function
  • Promotes longevity through mitochondrial support
