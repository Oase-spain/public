Frequently Asked

Your Questions,
Answered

How does IV therapy work?+
Intravenous therapy delivers nutrients, vitamins and compounds directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system entirely. This results in near-complete bioavailability — meaning your cells receive the full therapeutic dose rather than the fraction absorbed through oral supplementation. Treatments are administered through a small IV cannula under full medical supervision.
Why do I need blood tests first?+
Every person has a unique biochemical profile. Blood testing allows us to identify precise deficiencies, imbalances and optimization opportunities — ensuring your protocol is evidence-based and clinically appropriate. At OASE, we never administer a generic drip: your results determine your formulation.
Are treatments fully personalized?+
Completely. No two protocols at OASE are identical. Following your medical consultation and laboratory analysis, your prescribing physician designs a compound profile tailored exclusively to your biology, goals and current health status. Protocols are also adjusted over time as your biomarkers evolve.
How long does a session take?+
Infusion sessions typically range from 45 minutes to 90 minutes depending on your protocol. Express push shots take approximately 10 to 20 minutes. Your initial consultation and blood analysis appointment is separate and will be scheduled in advance of your first treatment.
Can IV therapy support athletic recovery?+
Yes. Intravenous delivery of amino acids, electrolytes, anti-inflammatory compounds and metabolic cofactors can significantly accelerate physical recovery compared to oral routes. Our Recovery and Titan protocols are specifically designed for athletic and high-performance contexts, always preceded by medical assessment.
Can protocols support menopause and hormonal wellness?+
Our Women's Wellness programme is designed specifically for the complex physiological changes of perimenopause and menopause. Through targeted micronutrient therapy based on laboratory findings, we support energy metabolism, cognitive function and hormonal balance — addressing the root biochemical causes of common symptoms.
What is NADH+ and is it available at OASE?+
NADH (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a critical coenzyme involved in cellular energy production, mitochondrial function and DNA repair — all central to healthy ageing. NADH+ infusion therapy is available exclusively at OASE and is administered following individual medical assessment. Please contact us directly to enquire about suitability and protocol options.
Who supervises my treatment?+
All protocols at OASE are prescribed by a licensed physician following medical consultation and biomarker analysis. IV administration is carried out by qualified clinical staff. Medical supervision is present throughout your session — your safety and comfort are our absolute priorities.
